What can WordPress be used for?

From advanced e-commerce stores and recruitment platforms to business websites, WordPress can be used in many different ways. It’s free, flexible, fully customisable and easy to use.

Content Management

At its core, WordPress is a content management system designed for creating, editing, organising, and publishing content on the web. It is easy to use, multimedia friendly and offer an almost unlimited range of 3rd party software integration options.

eCommerce

The introduction of WooCommerce has made adding eCommerce functionality to your WordPress website easier than ever. With the ability to take payments from the majority of payment providers, as well as manage stock and sell virtual or physical products and services.

Law Firms

In today’s competitive online market, having a high-performance website is crucial to the success of your law firm. A law firm website on the WordPress platform will play a key role in helping your practice attract and convert the right types of clients by performing well in the search engines and engaging visitors.

Business Websites

An estimated 20% of all websites are built on WordPress, that’s around 455,000,000 websites. With its unlimited range of design possibilities, robust security features, GDPR compliance, stability, and scalability it’s easy to see why so many businesses choose the WordPress platform for their business website.

Hotel Websites

A custom-built website from your hotel or restaurant on WordPress provides complete control over your branding and marketing channels. You’ll have all the tools you need to take bookings, manage availabilities and keep information up to date, with the ability to integrate with more third-party software than any other CMS.

Recruitment

WordPress offers an all-in-one solution for recruitment companies and employment agencies. Spend less time managing your website and more time placing candidates. Nurture your brand with thought leadership content alongside job listings, all managed through a single admin panel and integrated with your client management systems, job boards and social media platforms.

Add Ecommerce functionality to your WordPress website with WooCommerce.

From physical products and digital downloads to subscriptions, content and even appointments, you can sell anything with WooCommerce.

With an unrivalled number of integrations with payment gateways, shipping providers and accounting software, this is a great way to add an online shop to your brand’s website.
